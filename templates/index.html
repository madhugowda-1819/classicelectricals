Classic Electricals

Based in Bengaluru, Karnataka, Classic Electricals is a certified and trusted manufacturer and supplier of industrial-grade electrical instruments and advanced inspection equipment. With years of experience, we have built a strong reputation for delivering high-performance solutions to both B2B and B2C clients across India.

Our product line includes state-of-the-art Underwater Inspection Cameras, Gas Detectors, Video Borescopes, and Machine Vision Cameras. These solutions are designed to meet the stringent demands of industrial safety, monitoring, and inspection.

At Classic Electricals, we are committed to quality, innovation, and reliability. Each product is engineered with precision and tested for performance to ensure it meets the highest standards. Our team continuously strives to provide timely and effective solutions tailored to diverse industrial applications.

Whether you're looking for robust inspection tools or specialized electrical instruments, Classic Electricals delivers excellence that empowers your operations with confidence and safety.
